About

We Wellcome To Everyone

The excitement of curling, meandering drive at 4,700 feet is itself rewarding and awe inspiring. Just 35 minutes’ drive from the heart of Islamabad, RSH Resort is the sparkling eye of the nature. The journey towards the Resort is itself so inspiring and rewarding that it enhances the setting of the Resort, overlooking Daman-e-Koh. It stands out head and shoulders above others.

Set high and sprawling on the natural topography of the Margalla Hills on Pir Suhawa Road, Highland Resort is a unique recreational resort of its kind in Pakistan. A more 35 minutes’ drive from Islamabad, the Resort offers multiple and diversified kinds of interactive entertainment and recreational opportunities. A unique significance of the Resort is its proximity to Islamabad, a nearness which enables government officials to take a recreational break without taking leave. In contrast to Murree and Bhurban. Highland Resort’s proximity to Islamabad / Rawalpindi makes movement between the Resort and Islamabad/Rawalpindi easier and manageable.
